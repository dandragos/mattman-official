Emily Istrate

description

Not only beautiful, but also talented and charismatic, Emily Istrate represents a fresh presence in the music industry. The singer, songwriter and actress from the Republic of Moldova promises to conquer the world with her powerful talent. Emily has showcased her talent in numerous national and international competitions. The artist has made remarkable appearances on TV shows like The Voice Kids Ukraine (2016) and Next Star (2017), proving her talent in front of a wide audience from a very young age. With an unique style, a strong voice, and lyrics that you'll find yourself humming from the very first moment you hear them, she has gained international recognition with her first releases back in 2021 - “If You Want To” and “Panic”, both songs having reached top positions, ranking #4 and #5 in the UK Pop Club Charts. After winning over the international audience, the next step in Emily's impressive career was entering the Romanian music market, captivating the audience with her one-of-a-kind approach when it comes to every release. 2023 was the year which saw her climb to the top of all major Romanian music charts, as she put out “Bye Bye Boy” and “In Mintea Mea”, the latter being an absolute summer jam. With a large community on social media platforms, such as Instagram and TikTok, Emily constantly interacts with her followers, offering them a touch of inspiration and joy through her creative content, which allows her to showcase her incredible acting skills.
