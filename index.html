ABOUT US

Founded in 2015, Mattman Music is an independent Romanian record label representing the embodiment of Matteo’s desire to establish a creative hub that would foster the artistic evolution of young artists. Ever since its emergence in the music industry, Mattman has been striving to become a notable presence with remarkable contributions to the country’s music charts. The label’s roster includes names such as Matteo, Andrei Banuta, Emily Istrate, Gabi Bagu and Mara.
