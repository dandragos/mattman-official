Andrei Banuta

description

Andrei Banuta is one of the freshest voices to follow on the Romanian music scene. Having launched his career over 2 years ago, he solidified his status as one of the most in demand artists one song at a time. His songwriting has set him apart as a gifted young artist, his voice conveying emotions that instantly impact his listeners. His first major hit was the collaboration with Serena - “Mon Ami (RaTaTa)” - which quickly became a radio hit in Romania, also conquering radio charts in Turkey and India, gaining an incredible amount of popularity and receiving awards for the best pop song. Andrei’s greatest success, “Nu M-am Gandit la Despartire”, saw him collaborate with Romania’s favorite voice, Andra, on the remake of an absolute evergreen by Dan Spătaru. The song spent weeks on end at the top of all major radio charts and several weeks leading the YouTube Trending chart. More recently, “Afară Plouă”, his featuring song with DOMINO, has been extremely well-received not only by the large public, but also by all the significant radio stations, the track having been included in their playlists as soon as it was released. An artist of contrasts, he doesn’t shy away from alternating between deep, meaningful lyrics and vibrant, playful songs, the latter allowing him to explore a different side of his musical persona. Lately, he has been delving into acting, landing the role of Denis in one of the most major series on Romanian TV - Groapa, which has been the top ranked program ever since the first episode aired on PRO TV and VOYO, becoming a fan favorite right away. When it comes to his online presence, Andrei stands out as an artist who authentically presents all aspects of his life, offering his community sneak peeks at his latest projects and highlights of his concerts, but also starting meaningful conversations on the matters close to his heart.
