Mara

description

Having been signed exactly on her birthday by Mattman Music after taking part in a casting searching for new voices, Mara considered it to be a sign that music is her one true calling. After moving to Bucharest in order to pursue her dream, she had to start fresh in a new city, at a new high school, even changing her vocal coach, but she believes it was the best decision she could take. Her first single - “Nevermind” - was released in March this year, giving her a great head start, reaching around 250K views. Her second single - “Urmele” - an ultimate dance song, was released in late August, reaching 425K views on YouTube and being played on several radio stations, like Virgin Radio and Kiss FM. Knowing just how important public image is for an artist, Mara is passionate about clothes, she follows the latest trends and creations and manages to integrate them into her outfits, so that they represent her perfectly. Moreover, whenever she is not in the studio, she creates content for her social media pages, as it allows her to communicate more easily with the community she is building, showing them various aspects of her daily life, such as make-up routines, outfit checks or her travels.
